MindWave Assessment Lab

Welcome to the MindWave Assessment Lab, a dedicated space for understanding and improving your mental health. Here, you can take various well-recognized mental health assessments to gauge your current state and track your progress over time. Choose from the General Anxiety Disorder (GAD-7) Scale, the Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-9) Scale, and the Kessler Psychological Distress (K-10) Scale to get a comprehensive view of your mental well-being.

General Anxiety Disorder (GAD-7) Scale

The GAD-7 is a seven-item questionnaire that is used to measure or assess the severity of Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD). It is a simple, effective tool that helps individuals understand their anxiety levels by rating how often they've experienced certain symptoms over the past two weeks. Taking the GAD-7 can provide insights into whether you might benefit from further evaluation or professional support.

PATIENT HEALTH QUESTIONNAIRE (PHQ-9) Scale

The PHQ-9 is a nine-item scale that helps in identifying and measuring the severity of depression. Each item is based on the diagnostic criteria for major depressive disorder. The PHQ-9 is a valuable tool for screening, diagnosing, monitoring, and measuring the severity of depression. By regularly taking the PHQ-9, individuals can monitor their mental health status and seek timely intervention if needed.

Take PHQ-9 Test

Kessler Psychological Distress (K-10) Scale

The K-10 is a ten-item questionnaire intended to yield a global measure of distress based on questions about anxiety and depressive symptoms that a person has experienced in the most recent four-week period. The K-10 scale is used around the world and is highly effective in identifying individuals who require further psychological assessment. It helps in understanding the level of distress and in planning the next steps for managing mental health.
